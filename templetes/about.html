All about Shellter

Project “Shelter” is a startup idea. It’s a smart accommodation finding software which is infused with AI technology which will enable users to find their desired shelter within their personal preferences using just their smart phones, with integrated features such as VR view, ‎360° views, live feeds, multiple language support etc. Furthermore, direct user and seller/landlord interactions will be available through our chat. The objective is to provide more accommodations for rent or sale and also other benefits like temporary hotel stay at low cost will also be available.
The hassle of finding the right accommodation within your desired area and budget range will be greatly reduced; user friendliness will be our utmost priority.New finishes, appliances, and community features expand your opportunities at Oltera in SoEast. Enjoy tons of modern conveniences that make life easier (and way more fun).
